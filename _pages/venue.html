Where is the venue?

Address: La Rotonde de la Villette 6-8 Place de la Bataille de Stalingrad, 75019 Paris, France.

Open on OpenStreetMaps or Google Maps.

How to go there?

If you are coming from Charles de Gaulle Airport, you can take the RER B to Gare du Nord, then transfer to metro line 5 one stop to Stalingrad. It's also about a 15 minutes walk from Gare du Nord.

Nearby metro:

  • 2, 5 or 7 - Stalingrad
  • 2, 5 or 7bis - Jaurès

Buses:

  • 26, 48, 54 - Stalingrad

Where to stay?

Hotels near La Rotonde de la Villette:

Le Robinet d'Or is the closest hotel to the venue, and the most "Parisian." It is in a small, older building and the rooms are charming. This also means that there is no elevator, and the windows probably aren't completely soundproof. There's also very limited availability because it's a small hotel, so if this is your kind of place act fast.

Hotel Paris Louis Blanc is a much larger hotel that is around the corner from the venue. The best thing about this hotel is an interior garden that, in May, would be a lovely place to have breakfast. It's the furthest of the hotels on this list, but we're talking about an additional 2 minutes walk, max, from the venue.

Ibis Styles Gare de L’Est is right next to the train station — the rooms on one side of the hotel look out at the tracks. If you choose this hotel, the upper floors on the train track side also have a fabulous view of the Eiffel Tower and Sacre Coeur. The downside is there is likely some noise from the trains, but it's also a modern building so not likely too bad.
