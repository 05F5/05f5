Event Schedule

Event Format

This is a sample schedule to give you an idea of the event format — single track talks in the morning, breakout workshops in the afternoon. The schedule is subject to change.

Our goal is to make this event as concretely useful to open source founders as possible. We’re counting on input from you to figure out what your top challenges are and how the open source founder summit can help you conquer them.

If you’re interested in helping us co-create this event, request an invite and get in touch!
