Succession Planning

Or perhaps you want to step away to smell the roses, climb mountains, write a cookbook, or something else on your bucket list. But your FOSS business needs a plan to continue without your participation. What is your vision for your business without you at the helm? How do you plan to make that vision a reality?

As a programmer since 1970, although I am in good health, I want to ensure that YottaDB survives me. I think about it often. While we have a continuity plan, I confess to not having a succession plan.

If you are also concerned about the ongoing future of your FOSS business, please join our Succession Planning breakout workshop. Share your concerns, your ideas, and your plans. Let us learn from one another.
