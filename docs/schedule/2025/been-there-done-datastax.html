Talk

A Second-Time Founder’s Lessons on Building Open Source Companies

Monday, 19 May 2025 @ 09:40 - 10:00

DataStax CTO and co-founder Jonathan Ellis is now starting a new open source company after DataStax’s acquisition. In this talk, he’ll talk about the concrete things he’s planning to do differently at the new company, so you can learn from his experiences and apply the lessons from DataStax at your own company.
