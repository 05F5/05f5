Heili Toome

Global Engagement Director

MobilityData

Heili Toome is the Global Engagement Director at MobilityData, a non-profit organization that develops and governs open data standards for public transit and shared mobility, including GTFS and GBFS. She brings over 20 years of leadership and business strategy experience, with a specialization in shared mobility operations across North American cities.

Before joining MobilityData, Heili held operational leadership roles in the shared mobility sector, including work with bikeshare systems. MobilityData's open source tools, documentation, and data catalogs serve over 10,000 transit operators in more than 100 countries, advancing accessible and sustainable transportation through open standards.
