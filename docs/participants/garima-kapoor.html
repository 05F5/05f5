Open Source Founders Summit 2025
May 19-20
Paris France

Speaker info

Garima Kapoor

Co-founder & Co-CEO

MinIO

Open Source is the future of software. We see it in AI, we see it in the enterprise. The key challenge for many entrepreneurs is understanding how to create ongoing value from open source - for the community, for the company or project and for the stakeholders in that company/project. That is not a straightforward proposition and there are plenty of opinions. The Open Source Founders Summit is a great forum to engage in this conversation - openly, transparently and with a diversity of voices.
