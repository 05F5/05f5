Building a profitable open source company is hard, and the path is rarely linear — and AI is now rewriting the
rules under everyone's feet. In this talk, Pierre Burgy, co-founder and CEO of Strapi, shares the unfiltered
story of how Strapi managed to turn things around and reach profitability, and why founders who don't disrupt
themselves risk being disrupted by AI instead.

Drawing from Strapi's own experience Pierre will cover the trade-offs founders face when balancing community
growth, product investment, and financial discipline, and how to position an open source business to ride the
AI wave rather than be flattened by it.
