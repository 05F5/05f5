Being a founder is hard, but having fun is probably the most underrated lever in a founder's toolkit. Founders who 
are having fun build better products, recover from setbacks faster, and stay in the game longer. In this session, 
we'll share the personal, practical tips and routines that have actually moved the needle for us in reclaiming the 
fun in the work.
