How can open source projects promote each other's work? Anna kicks off this workshop with a concrete example: MariaDB is launching the [MariaDB Ecohub](https://ecohub.mariadb.org/), a directory of products that work well with MariaDB, paired with "solution cards" that combine a few of them into ready-to-use bundles. From there, the conversation opens up to other forms of collaboration — cross-promoting content, sharing audiences, and any other ideas the room wants to explore.
