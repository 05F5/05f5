We managed to secure around €1.5M in funding to accelerate innovation in Drupal AI. This workshop explores the 
practical side of building a fundable open source initiative by aligning contributors, companies, and communities 
around a shared vision. 

Using the Drupal AI and Dropsolid AI experiences as case studies, we’ll look at topics such as:
- Positioning open source initiatives so companies want to fund them
- Structuring sponsorship and contribution models
- Aligning commercial incentives with community goals
- Building credibility and momentum around emerging technology initiatives
