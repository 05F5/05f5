I lead teams that build data tools at DataKind, a global nonprofit using data science, machine learning, and AI in the service of humanity. I'm passionate about making information accessible and actionable in order to help drive equitable outcomes and empower community-led action.

Caitlin is Vice President at DataKind, a global nonprofit that harnesses the power of data science and AI in the service of humanity. Founded in 2012, DataKind designs scalable, data-driven tools that address some of the world's toughest challenges, ranging from frontline health to community-led action.
