John O'Nolan is the founder and CEO of Ghost, an open-source publishing platform designed for independent journalists, writers, and creators. Before starting Ghost, he worked as a designer and developer, including a stint as Deputy Head of Design at WordPress. O'Nolan created Ghost to offer a simpler, more focused alternative to traditional content management systems. Today, it powers thousands of websites worldwide and operates as a non-profit organization.
