Alexandre is the CEO of Rudder, an open-source continuous configuration solution. Rudder is a system infrastructure automation software that provides configuration and security management solutions. Rudder is designed to continuously control the compliance of hybrid and complex infrastructures, for a fast, reliable and efficient management.

With a background in cybersecurity, Alexandre previously worked for several years at several French integrators and IT services companies to ensure production security. Alexandre also speaks and volunteer at conferences such as Pass the SALT.
