Amandine is a co-founder and Guardian of the Matrix.org Foundation. The Foundation is the custodian for the Matrix open standard, a unique initiative aiming to democratise secure online communication and solve the problem of fragmentation in current Chat, VoIP, VR and IoT technologies. Matrix makes open real-time-communication as universal and interoperable as email.

Amandine is also COO and co-founder for Element, the company building the eponym open source, secure and interoperable collaboration app built on Matrix. Element also delivers Matrix solutions to millions of users, including the French Government, the German Armed Forces, and private companies like Mozilla.
