I'm the lead developer and creator of webrecorder. Passionate about web archiving technologies and open source.

Ilya Kreymer is the founder and lead developer of Webrecorder, a suite of open source tools for high-fidelity web archiving. In 2014 he started development on Webrecorder.io, the first browser-based interactive web archiving tool, and on pywb, the web archive replay system that powers many archival projects today.

He also created Oldweb-Today, a project for browsing the historical web through emulated browsers, and contributes to the International Internet Preservation Consortium (IIPC). His work focuses on building open infrastructure for capturing and replaying the web at scale.
