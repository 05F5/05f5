Ilya Kreymer is the founder and lead developer of Webrecorder, a company dedicated to building and maintaining a suite of open source high-fidelity web archiving tools that allow anyone to create, access and share archives of complex websites. Webrecorder is currently focused on building Browsertrix, a fully open SaaS service that supports web archiving at scale, as well as continuing to maintain a portfolio of open source tools used by individuals, researchers, libraries and companies around the world. Ilya has been working on open source tools since at least 2013.
