Taco is the co-Founder & CEO of Open Social and active in the open-source space (mainly Drupal) since 2008. Before that Taco created a digital agency GoalGorilla that turned into the open-SaaS company Open Social in 2019. Funded through a crowdfunding and seed round, the startup has been profitable since 2023.
