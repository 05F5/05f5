Stefano Maffulli is the Executive Director of the Open Source Initiative (OSI), where he leads efforts to promote and protect open source software. He has a long background in open source advocacy, including contributions to GNU, OpenStreetMap, and WordPress, and earlier leadership at the Free Software Foundation Europe. At OSI, he focuses on adapting open source principles to new challenges, including artificial intelligence.
