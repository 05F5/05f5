Marc is a social entrepreneur with a focus on Free / Libre / Open Source software, wikis, collaboration, machine learning, information governance, digital transformation and organizational performance. Marc is the Founder of WikiSuite.org While Wikipedia is the broadest unified body of knowledge, WikiSuite is the most comprehensive and integrated Open Source enterprise solution.

Marc is also CEO of EvoluData.com, which offers integrated communication, collaboration and commerce solutions for enterprise departments.
