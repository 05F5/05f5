Nithya Ruff was the Head of Open Source Program Office at AWS, and chair of the Linux Foundation board of directors.

Nithya Ruff is known for her work in the open-source community, particularly as a leader and advocate for open source within various organizations. She has held positions in several companies, including Comcast, SanDisk, and Western Digital, where she focused on open source strategy and community engagement.
