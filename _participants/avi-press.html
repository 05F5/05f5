My work at Scarf is all about helping other open source businesses be more successful. I'm excited for OSFS to meet more open source founders, learn from them, and better understand the challenges they are facing.

Avi is the founder and CEO of Scarf. Scarf builds advanced user & customer intelligence for open source software projects. On top of being an open source maintainer and advocate, Avi is also a functional programming enthusiast. Prior to Scarf, Avi was an engineer at Pandora.
