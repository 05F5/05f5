Stephen Millidge is the Founder and CEO of Payara, a company dedicated to providing reliable open source software for enterprise Java environments. He has extensive experience building sustainable open source businesses and fostering strong developer communities. Stephen is a regular advocate for open standards, open collaboration, and practical strategies for growing open source projects commercially.
