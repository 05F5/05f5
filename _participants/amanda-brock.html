Amanda Brock is CEO of OpenUK, the UK organisation for the business of Open Technology with a purpose of UK Leadership and Global Collaboration in Open Technology. She is also the Executive Producer of State of Open Con.

Amanda is also a member of a number of Open Source and Open Standards Boards, a keynote speaker and author. Amanda writes regularly for the tech press and is the editor of Open Source Law, Policy and Practice.
