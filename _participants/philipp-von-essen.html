Philipp co-founded Knowledge Pixels, which aims to boost the sharing of scientific findings based on an open source platform. They are building an open and decentralized ecosystem for Knowledge Graph snippets where insights are connected and come with rich provenance. This enables fast and new ways to publish and access to what researchers discover. With a background in business administration and economics, he focuses on business development and marketing, as well as handling financial and legal matters for Knowledge Pixels.
