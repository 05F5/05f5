Getting open source business founders together is a great way to fosters a supportive network where we can share our experiences and learn from each other's mistakes. It's all about helping each other grow and succeed in the open-source world.

Kevin Muller is the CEO of Passbolt, a leading open-source password manager designed for collaboration. With a background in software development and a passion for cybersecurity, Kevin leads Passbolt with a vision of empowering organizations to secure their digital assets effectively. Before joining Passbolt, Kevin founded ClickOnFrench, an e-learning platform, and actively contributed to several open-source projects while running his web agency in India.
