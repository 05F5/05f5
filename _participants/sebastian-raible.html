Sebastian Raible is Policy Director at APELL, the European Open Source Software Business Association. He focuses on the intersection of open source, technology policy, and regulation within the European Union. His work supports open source businesses in navigating legislative changes such as the Digital Services Act, the Cyber Resilience Act, and the AI Act, while advocating for open source interests across European institutions.
