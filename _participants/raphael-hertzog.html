Raphaël Hertzog is a long-time Debian developer and founder of Freexian, a company specializing in Debian consulting and long-term support. He has contributed to key infrastructure tools like the Debian Package Tracker and co-authored the Debian Administrator’s Handbook. Through Freexian, he supports sustainable open source development by funding Debian contributors and maintaining critical packages.
