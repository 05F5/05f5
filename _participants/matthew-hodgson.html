Matthew Hodgson is the co-founder and CEO of Element, as well as the technical co-founder of the Matrix.org Foundation. Matrix is an open standard for decentralised, encrypted real-time communication, powering deployments for organisations including the German Armed Forces, NATO, and the French government. Matthew has spent over two decades building communication platforms, driven by a mission to give users sovereignty over their data.
