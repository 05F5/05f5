Jordan Maris is the EU Policy Analyst at the Open Source Initiative (OSI), where he serves as a vital link between European Union legislators and the open source community. In this role, he monitors EU policy developments, educates lawmakers on the benefits of open source, and advocates for its integration into legislation, particularly concerning digital and AI policies.
