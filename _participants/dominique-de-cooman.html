Dominique De Cooman is the co-founder and CTO of Dropsolid, a company specializing in open-source Digital Experience Platforms (OpenDXP). For over a decade, he has guided companies, organizations, and governments through digital transformation and AI adoption, advocating for open DXP solutions that bring together Drupal, Mautic, and emerging AI frameworks. He serves on the Drupal Association Board and the Mautic Council.

Dominique is a recognized open-source advocate and a leader in the Drupal AI initiative. He has been an active contributor to the Drupal community since 2009 and was elected to the Drupal Association Board in 2024. He passed the CEO role at Dropsolid to Carlo Schots in 2025, while remaining the owner of the Dropsolid holding and maintaining a strong connection with the community.
