Adam Jacob is the co-founder and CEO of System Initiative, a company reimagining DevOps tooling through real-time, collaborative infrastructure modeling. Prior to this, he co-founded Chef, a configuration management tool, where he served as CTO and significantly influenced modern infrastructure automation practices. Jacob is recognized for his innovative contributions to DevOps and infrastructure management.
