Ann Schlemmer is the CEO of Percona, a company specializing in open-source database software, support, and services. She joined Percona in 2013 and has held various leadership roles, including President and General Manager. Before her tenure at Percona, Schlemmer was part of the MySQL Professional Services team at Sun Microsystems and later Oracle.
