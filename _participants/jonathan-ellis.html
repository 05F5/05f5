Jonathan Ellis is the co-founder and former CTO of DataStax, a company that develops database solutions based on Apache Cassandra. He was also a Project Chair for Apache Cassandra, contributing to its growth as a leading distributed database. Ellis is recognized for his work in building scalable, high-performance data systems and his contributions to open-source technology.
