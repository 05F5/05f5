Sandro Groganz is a specialist in open source marketing and community development currently head of marketing at Passbolt. In the past, he has helped open source companies like eZ Systems and Mindquarry improve their communication and outreach, and founded CampaignChain, an open source tool for managing digital marketing campaigns.
