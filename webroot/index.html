Open Source Founder Summit 2024
May 27-28
Paris France

The conference about building financially successful and sustainable open source companies.

Your backstage pass to the world of open source businesses.

So, you’re into the wild ride of building an open source company? Did you ever feel that other startup founders & investors can't grasp your real struggles and wins? Do you think open source companies should be collaborating more with each other?

You are not alone. This is why we are carving out a space where open source leaders can spill the beans. Get ready for a no-nonsense vibe, candid exchange of hard-learned lessons, successful game plans, and a whole lot of open source wisdom.

About

How will #OSFS24 help me grow?

Meet your peers, share your metrics, receive & give feedback, and get unstuck.

Form lasting connections: Build relationships with industry leaders and peers.

Benchmark your performance: Discuss stats relevance and see how they measure up, and how to improve them.

Gain new skills: Get concrete strategies and tactics to try out in your business.

Get unstuck: Get input from founders of more mature companies to figure out the best course of action.
